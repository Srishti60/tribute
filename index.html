Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, TamilNadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.

About the Legend

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam's full name is Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam is also known as Missile Man.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was born in the Dhanushkodi village of Rameswaram which is now located in Tamil Nadu.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam belonged to a poor family and used to sell newspapers to complete his studies.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam completed his post-graduation in space science from the Madras Institute of Technology.

  • After completing his studies, Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam started working as an aerospace engineer in the Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO).

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam Joined ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization) in 1962 and got involved in satellite launch activities. Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was also the President of India from 2002 to 2007.

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam died on 27 July 2015 during a lecture in Shillong Meghalaya.
